About

I'm Book'd is a business created by a book lover, for other book lovers.First and foremost we love reading, which means we love buying new books, so of course we have a section for you to find new titles to add to your personal libraries. We also wanted to create somewhere for you to be able to connect with other book readers, and what better way to do so than by competing in reading challenges with them? Last, but not least we understand how hard it can be to find a new book when you're still recovering from the ending of the last one; so we have lots of ranked lists of books that are voted on by other readers, that you can also vote on.

We hope you have as much fun exploring our website, as you do exploring new worlds inside your books.
